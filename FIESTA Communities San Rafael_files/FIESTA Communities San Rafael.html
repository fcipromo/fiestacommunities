SAN RAFAEL

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Spanning an expansive 10 hectares, FIESTA Communities San Rafael Extension shines as a beacon of hope, turning the dreams of Filipino families in Tarlac City and nearby towns into reality by offering safe and affordable homes. Its strategic location, with excellent connectivity to major roads and essential establishments, further sets this community apart.
